ABOUT US

Vel's Construction Singapore (2020) Pte Ltd is a construction company that specialises in residential landed properties.

We are registered with the Building and Construction Authority of Singapore (BCA) as a Class 1 builder. From consultation to completion, we provide turn key services to all our valued clients. With a team of young and vibrant individuals working together as a close knit team, we strive to serve beyond construction of a template house with four walls and a roof. We aim to conceptualise new home designs that suit our clients' need and yet make their house the highlight of their street.

At Vel's, we are a builder that builds with heart. We build dreams into reality and turns houses into homes. We hope every valued client will move into their new home filled with love. We pride ourselves in quality workmanship, ethical practice, on-time delivery and constant pursuit towards value-quality satisfaction. Our commitment to upholding these values build trusts in returning clients who choose to work with us.
